Blueberry Pie

Description

What's better than a delicious blueberry pie for dessert? If you've never made crust from scratch, have no fear! This recipe will guide you through the steps required to make a delicious, flaky crust.

Ingredients

Pie Crust:
  • 2 cups plus 2 tbsp. all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2/3 cup shortening
  • 6 to 8 tbsp. ice cold water
Filling:
  • 6 cups fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp. cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp. lemon juice
  • 1 tsp. lemon zest

Steps

  1. Combine flour and salt. Cut in shortening until evenly dispersed.
  2. Add cold water and mix. Dough should cling together and clear sides of bowl.
  3. Separate into two balls, cover and put in the fridge while you prepare the filling.
  4. Stir the blueberries, sugar, flour, cornstarch, cinnamon, lemon juice, and lemon zest together in a large bowl. Mix until it is no longer dry and powdery.
  5. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Place baking sheet on bottom oven rack to catch any blueberry juices.
  6. Roll out the chilled pie dough. Fold in half and then half again and carefully transfer to pie plate. Unfold and center.
  7. Pour filling into pie.
  8. Roll out the second ball of dough. Cut into strips to make a lattice.
  9. Assemble the lattice on top of the filling. Place strips vertically until pie is covered. Weave in horizontal strips by going under and over the vertical strips.
  10. Bake the pie at 425 degrees for 25 mins., then reduce temperature to 375 degrees. Place tin foil around the edges of the pie to prevent over-browning. Bake for another 40-50 mins.
  11. Remove pie from oven, and cool before serving.
