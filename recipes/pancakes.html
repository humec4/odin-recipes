Pancakes

Description

My favourite go-to pancake recipe. They come out delicious and fluffy every time! A little melted butter in the batter is the secret ingredient

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 heaping tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well in the center and add milk, egg and melted butter.
  3. Mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly-oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Scoop batter onto the pan. Cook until bubbles form and edges are dry.
  6. Flip. Cook until both sides are browned.
  7. Remove from heat and repeat directions with remaining batter.
